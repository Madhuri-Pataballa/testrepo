San Juan Islands

Where..?
The San Juan Islands are an archipelago in the northwest corner of the contiguous United States between the US mainland and Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Canada. The San Juan Islands are part of the U.S. state of Washington.
About San Juan Island..
San Juan Island is the second-largest and most populous of the San Juan Islands in northwestern Washington, United States. It has a land area of 142.59 km² and a population of 6,822 as of the 2000 census.
How did we stranded in an island..?
Actually we went there for a vacation. We rented a motor boat for 3 days. We were almost there, and unfortunately we had a problem with the boat. We some how managed to get there, as we are almost at our final destination point. There was no means of any communication.
But it was real fun. We enjoyed every moment. As we took everything with us including food and other stuff, we couldn't find it hard. On the 2nd day, another family came in for camping. They helped us to come through it.
We took camping gear, food, water, cooking kit and some blankets along with medical first-aid kit.
